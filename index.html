The local bottleneck

The waste is not always leftovers. Sometimes it is food that was never wanted.

In a fast lunch line, a student grabs milk, fruit, or a sealed side because it is required, convenient, or sitting before the cashier. Minutes later it returns untouched.

Second Bell treats that moment as a rescue window, not a trash report. It connects lunch-line behavior, cold storage, share tables, and after-school snack demand in one local decision.

01

Selected

A meal component is taken during service, often as a default item.

02

Returned

The item comes back unopened or whole at the tray return station.

03

Timed

A cold-chain or supervision deadline determines whether rescue is still possible.

04

Routed

Eligible items move to a monitored share table or approved after-school demand.

Input -> AI -> insight -> action

A lightweight AI stack built for one cafeteria decision.

Inputs

Morning setup

Menu, weather, event tag, attendance, line setup, cooler capacity, monitor availability, and after-school demand.

AI

Ghost forecast

Classifier, quantile regressors, demand matcher, anomaly detector, and rule-constrained state machine.

Output

Action cards

Deploy by time, recovery range, safety gate, labor load, stockout risk, and manager approval.
